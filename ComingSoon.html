Coming Soon

The following features are on our agenda for the official Melatect app. They will be available soon! The current BETA version does not include the updates on this page. These updates may or may not be at a cost to the user. We recommend you check these features out before suggesting new ones in case we already have your suggestion on our mind!

Personal Accounts and Data Safety

Personal accounts will allow users to log in and out of the app and save data in case of app deletion. This feature is currently unavailable for Melatect BETA but will be coming soon.

February 2021

In-app Dermatologist Review

We are currently working on providing freelance dermatologist consultation that users can opt for in-app. This feature will cost less than the average co-pay for an in-person dermatologist visit and will be entirely virtual, providing a quicker, more convenient, COVID-free environment by which users can get moles checked out safely and inexpensively.

April 2021

Progression Tracking

Progression tracking is a feature where users can take photos of their moles and watch how they've progressed over time. Change in appearance and other variables over time is a common indicator of melanoma for at-risk patients. Users will be able to take photos of their moles and be given a progressive analysis of their mole.

May 2021
